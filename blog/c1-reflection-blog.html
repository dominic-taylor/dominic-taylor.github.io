What's your take on the DBC / EDA experience?

I think it is very well put together. I like the focus on things other than coding and the staff I have met seem very genuine and committed.

What are your impressions?

The two thoughts that come to mind, is caring and intense.

How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

I can seem myself really enjoying it but also at the same time I like my own time so being around a lot of outgoing people could be a challenge.

Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

To be honest my expectations haven't changed that much. I am pretty cautious and really research a lot, before I have to commit to something requiring the investment of EDA. That being said it's the first week! I'm prepared to be surprised.

Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

I'm very excited! I see it as a chance push myself and learn a whole new skill that will serve me well.
